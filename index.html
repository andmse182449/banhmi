Our Story

At Hello BanhMi, we believe a sandwich is never just a sandwich. In Vietnam, bánh mì is more than a street food staple — it's a cultural icon. A delicious collision of East and West, it's where crispy French baguettes meet bold Vietnamese flavors like pickled veggies, pâté, BBQ meats, fresh herbs, and fiery chili.

Bánh mì is what you grab on a busy morning, share with friends after school, or take on a road trip — it's comfort food, wrapped in paper, ready to go.

We grew up with it, and now we're bringing it to Crescent Heights with a modern twist — pairing it with bubble tea, kimbap, coffee, and even tanghulu (sugar-coated fruit on a stick!). Whether you're Vietnamese or just bánh mì-curious, we're here to serve you something delicious, fast, and full of heart.

Come hungry. Leave happy.
